About Me

I'm Divya Jain, an AI/ML Engineer and Computer Science student at Jaypee University of Engineering and Technology, with real-world experience building machine learning systems that go beyond notebooks and actually ship.

I've spent the last year building at companies like TeamLease RegTech and Trustique Assist — designing compliance intelligence systems, recommendation engines, and scalable data pipelines. I love the full arc of a problem: understanding the data, choosing the right model, and wiring it into a backend that other people can actually use.

Outside of work, I compete. Hackathons, DSA contests, XR challenges — they keep me sharp and remind me that the best ideas come from building fast and iterating faster. I also lead technical workshops as Secretary of my university's VR/AR/MR Club and run coding events as Vice Chairperson of the GeeksforGeeks Student Chapter, because I believe what you learn is only half the story — sharing it is the other half.

My motto? "Build it. Break it. Make it better."
